# UNANIMO Game Rules

## Overview
* **Players**: 3 to 12 players
* **Goal**: Score points by matching the "thoughts" (words) of other players.

## Equipment
* 110 Unanimo cards

## Setup
1. Each player takes a score sheet and a pencil, then writes their name at the top.
2. Shuffle the 110 cards and place them face down in a center pile.
3. Choose a starting player.

## How to Play
1. **Reveal**: The active player turns over the top card and places it face up for all to see.
2. **Write**: All players write 8 words in the first column of their sheet that relate to the picture on the card.
3. **Time**: The first player allows a "reasonable" amount of time for everyone to finish. (Note: It is okay if a player has fewer than 8 words).
4. **Scoring**:
    * The first player reads their first word aloud.
    * Everyone who has that exact word (including the reader) raises their hand.
    * Each of those players scores points equal to the number of hands raised. 
    * If no one else has the word, the player scores 0 points.
    * This repeats for all 8 words on the first player's list.
    * Then, the next player (clockwise) reads any words on their list that haven't been mentioned yet.
